StoryLens – Privacy Policy

Effective: 3 Oct 2025

StoryLens is a Google Docs helper extension that analyzes the text of the document you have open and keeps a local “story bible.” We designed it to collect the minimum data needed to provide these features.

What data we access

  • Website content (Google Docs text) – When you click actions like “Analyze Story,” StoryLens reads the text of your current document. For more accurate export, you may grant read-only scopes to Google Docs and Drive.
  • OAuth token – Obtained via chrome.identity to call Google’s read-only APIs. The token is managed by Chrome; we do not receive or store it on our servers.

What data we do not access

  • No emails, contacts, calendar, or browsing history.
  • No keystroke logging or tracking across websites.
  • No advertising identifiers.

How data is used

  • On your device – The “story bible” (entities, threads, settings) is stored locally using Chrome storage. You can clear it anytime from the extension’s options or by clearing site data.
  • Server processing (when you click actions like Analyze/Structure/Chat) – The selected text is sent over HTTPS to our backend solely to return the requested result. We do not sell or share your text for advertising.
  • We use OpenAI’s API to generate analysis. Your text may be transmitted to OpenAI as a processor to fulfill your request. See OpenAI’s privacy & data use policies for details.

Retention

  • Document text – Not stored by us after the request completes. We may keep minimal server logs (timestamp, request path, IP) for abuse prevention and reliability.
  • Feedback you submit (optional “Accept/Reject” buttons) – Stored as small JSONL entries (issue id, your note) to improve suggestions. You may request deletion (see Contact).

Data sharing

  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not use your data for purposes unrelated to the extension’s single purpose.
  • Sub-processors: Hosting provider (for serving the API) and OpenAI (for model inference).

Your choices

  • Use the extension without Google OAuth (reduced accuracy), or click “Connect Google” to grant read-only access to the active Doc.
  • Revoke access anytime at Google Account Permissions.
  • Clear local data via Chrome → Clear browsing data or by removing the extension.

Security

All communications with our servers use HTTPS. Tokens managed via chrome.identity are not transmitted to us.

Children

StoryLens is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. We will update the “Effective” date above.
